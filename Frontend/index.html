7th Tobit.Campus Days

Since 2014, they have been a fixture in the annual calendar: The Ahauser Campus Days. The "place to be" for all who want to know more. Thousands of attendees, the best speakers in the country and lots of practical knowledge, the 7th edition of the 22nd - March has everything in the luggage. The focus of Campus Days is probably the most recent and most important.

The digital revolution is changing the world, at a speed that leaves many astonished. The Ahaus Campus Days are the reason for this revolution. The best coaches and trainers in the country cover what is behind the digitization, give courage and give valuable tips for their own, digital way. The workshops with many practical examples help to understand which building blocks are important and how they can be used profitably for themselves and their company.

The all-inclusive tickets include, in addition to the access to the different sessions, all the conference drinks, snacks and a nice participant package. They are available for a short time at the early bird price of 29 euros for the day ticket and 79 euros for the entire event.

Time March 22, 2017 - 10:00 AM - March 24, 2017 - 06:00 PM
Location Tobit.Campus, 48683 Ahaus, Germany
